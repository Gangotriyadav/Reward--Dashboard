What is reward?

something that is given in return for good or evil done or received or that is offered or given for some service or attainment.

How can I claim a reward?

Points are based on academic performance, cultural contributions, attendance, sessions conducted, placements, and participation in programs.

How can I claim a reward?

If a reward is available and your house is eligible, click the “Claim” button on the dashboard. Some rewards may require mentor approval.

Why are some rewards shown in grayscale?

Grayscale rewards are either already claimed or currently unavailable for your house or campus.
